Reward points system — Rule Book

A catalog of policies governing Customer accounts, System Administration, and Marketing representative referrals.

Customer Policies

  • Gets a 7-day credit period from invoice date to pay.
  • Pays within 7 days → double reward points (2.00x multiplier).
  • Pays after 7 days → points gradually decrease with each delayed day.
  • Pays after 30 days → zero points awarded.
  • Buys ₹2,00,000+ in a month → extra bonus points.
  • Buys above ₹2,00,000 again next month → additional loyalty bonus.
  • Buys every month consistently → regular customer bonus.
  • Buys above ₹2,00,000 → extra points scaled to purchase value.
  • Buys a Special Product or Old Stock item → automatic bonus points added on top of normal points (Special: +300 / Old Stock: +500, stacking to +800 if both are on one invoice).
  • Referred by marketing rep → still gets all normal rewards, doubled if paid within 7 days.

System Administrator Policies

  • Designates products as Special Product or Old Stock.
  • Sets bonus point value per tagged product (e.g. Special Product = +300, Old Stock = +500).
  • System auto-scans every invoice for tagged items and applies the bonus automatically.
  • Bonuses from multiple tagged items on one invoice accumulate (e.g., Special + Old Stock = +800).
  • Owns core thresholds driving the engine: 7-day credit, 30-day cutoff, and ₹2,00,000 monthly limit.

Marketing Policies

  • Rep earns commission on every purchase made through them (no minimum purchase requirement).
  • Rep earns 1% of invoice value in reward points.
  • Onboarding a new shop → Rep receives a flat bonus of 1,000 points upon successful customer verification. This is a one-time onboarding reward in addition to standard sales points.
  • Redemption Channels → Representatives can redeem their points balance directly for **cash transfers** (1 point = ₹1 bank transfer) or choose from premium catalog products (Bikes, Laptops, TVs, Phones).
  • Rep's points are credited only after successful invoice payment completed by the customer.
  • Customer on the referred invoice still gets full normal rewards independently (doubled if paid within 7 days).

Core Business Rules (BR-01 to BR-14)

  • BR-01: Set 7-day payment credit period.
  • BR-02: Payment within 7 days doubles reward points.
  • BR-03: Points decrease daily after day 7.
  • BR-04: Zero points after 30 days.
  • BR-05: ₹2,00,000+ monthly purchases earn bonus points.
  • BR-06: Consecutive ₹2,00,000+ monthly purchases earn loyalty bonus.
  • BR-07: Monthly purchasing earns regular customer bonus.
  • BR-08: Purchases over ₹2,00,000 earn additional value-based points.
  • BR-09: Admin flags Special Product/Old Stock.
  • BR-10: Product bonuses add to normal purchase points.
  • BR-11: Multiple items in one invoice yield cumulative bonuses.
  • BR-12: Representatives earn 1% of invoice value for all purchases made through them (no minimum purchase required).
  • BR-13: Representative points credit post-payment.
  • BR-14: Customers earn points per standard payment rules.
  • BR-15: Representatives receive a one-time flat bonus of 1,000 points for onboarding a new shop customer.

What the Admin Side Controls
01
Reward Rule Configuration
  • Set the payment credit period (e.g., 7 days)
  • Set the zero-point cutoff window (e.g., 30 days)
  • Set the monthly high-purchase bonus threshold amount
  • Set marketing referral minimum invoice value and reward rate (1%)
  • Every setting is dynamic and configurable
02
Product Tagging
  • Mark any inventory item as Special Product or Old Stock
  • Designate specific products for Double Points (2.0x multiplier)
  • Assign unique bonus point values per tagged item
  • System auto-scans invoices for tags with zero manual steps
  • Multiple tagged item bonuses accumulate additively
03
Config Versioning
  • Save changes as new configurations with timestamped revisions
  • Historic invoices evaluate strictly against rules active at purchase time
  • Changes today never retroactively overwrite yesterday's point credits
04
Audit Log
  • Complete ledger containing customer IDs, rule, timestamp, invoice
  • Filterable by rule types, customers, and invoice identifiers
  • Full historic traceability for point disputes and auditing
05
Marketing Rep Oversight
  • Monitor representatives and the active invoices linked to them
  • Track pending vs. finalized commission credits (1% pool)
  • Approve and log one-time onboarding bonuses (+1,000 pts) for newly registered shops
  • Identify which invoices qualify vs. don't
06
Reports & Monitoring
  • Evaluate points issued by period, customer tiers, and product tags
  • Identify accounts approaching the high purchase spend limit
  • Consolidate commission payouts by representative and month
